ABOUT PSGR KRISHNAMMAL COLLEGE FOR WOMEN

College Profile

PSGR Krishnammal College for Women was established in the year 1963 under the GRG Trust. It is an autonomous institution affiliated with Bharathiar University, Coimbatore. The college has grown into one of India's leading institutions for higher education for women, with a strong focus on academic excellence, research, innovation, and holistic development.

Vision
To empower women through quality education, innovation, leadership, and lifelong learning while contributing to society with knowledge and values.
Mission
  • Provide quality higher education.
  • Promote research and innovation.
  • Develop leadership and entrepreneurial skills.
  • Encourage ethical values and social responsibility.
  • Prepare students for global careers.
